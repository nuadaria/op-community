Hordelands

Stretching across the horizon, the Hordelands are a wild and unforgiving landscape, characterized by rolling plains, jagged canyons, and barren stretches of scorched earth.

Shadowed Wastes

The Shadowed Wastes, a desolate and foreboding expanse, stand as a grim testament to the enduring darkness that lingers from the Dark Tide.

Blighted Marshes

Once vibrant wetlands teeming with diverse flora and fauna, the Blighted Marshes now exude an aura of desolation and decay.
